# Clinic Payroll Calculator Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 25, 2026

Clinic Payroll Calculator is designed to run entirely in your browser. It processes CSV or Excel files that you choose from SimplePractice exports to calculate clinic income, clinician pay rules, and pay-period statements.

## Data Processed

The extension may process names of clinicians and clients, appointment dates, billing or CPT codes, payment sources, payment amounts, payer names, payment references, clinician pay rules, and generated payroll results. The exact data depends on the CSV or Excel files you select.

## Data Storage

The extension stores imported file draft data and clinician pay rules only in Chrome local extension storage on your device. The Reset button clears imported file draft data and current results. Saved clinician pay rules can remain in local extension storage until you clear extension data or uninstall the extension.

## Data Sharing

The extension does not transmit data to any external server. It does not use analytics, advertising, tracking pixels, remote code, or third-party APIs. Data is not sold, rented, or shared.

## Permissions

The `storage` permission is used to save local pay rules and draft imports between side panel sessions. The `unlimitedStorage` permission is used so larger CSV or Excel exports can be stored locally without Chrome's default extension storage quota interrupting payroll work. The `sidePanel` permission is used to show the calculator in Chrome's persistent browser side panel.

## Contact

For privacy questions, contact the extension publisher through the support information listed on the Chrome Web Store listing.
